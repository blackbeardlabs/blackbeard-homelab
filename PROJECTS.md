## Entry guidance

- Add projects when they use the local AI setup in a meaningful way.
- Link to the app repository or demo when available.
- Mention which model, backend, and node contributed to the work.
- Keep notes factual: what was built, what worked, what was difficult, and what changed in the lab setup.
- Avoid storing app source code, build output, or generated dependencies in this repository.
